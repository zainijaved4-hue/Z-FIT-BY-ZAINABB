Z Fit by Zainab was born from a simple belief: every person deserves access to world-class fitness guidance, regardless of where they're starting from. Founded in 2018 by Zainab Khan, our gym is a space where dedication meets compassion.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. We craft individualised journeys — blending strength training, mindful movement, and smart nutrition — to help you unlock your best self, sustainably.
